About

My name is Shadi Mokhtar. Over the last few years I made a name for myself by building interactive experiences and great brands. I'm a fan of simplicity and data-driven designs. I’ve worked on many projects for all sorts of clients, and almost always with a mobile-first mindset. Starting with creative concept, strategy developments, UI/UX Design , marketing as well as bridging communication between developers and designers. I'm also passionate about Ilustrations and filmmaking.

I started my career as a a self-taught visual designer in Mansoura, Egypt. Afterwards, I joined Information Technology Institue in Cairo where I developed my passion for multimedia and motion design. Shortly after, I was proud to work at vimov LLC on Clear Day® - Weather HD (formerly), a stunning weather app for mobile phones and tables and on many amazing elearning projects at MGD Computer Systems.I'm currently a UX Designer at Booking.com in Amsterdam.

My long term goal is using technology to make our lives better and more meaningful.
